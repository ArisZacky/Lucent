LUCENT EAU DE PARFUME

Our name Lucent means Radiant, Luminous. Radiance is often associated with allure and attractiveness. A person described as radiant is seen as glowing with charm and charisma. Luminous suggests captivating, and visually stunning quality; often symbolizing positivity, rich, and versatile aura related to light and glow.

Our fundamental conviction is that each of our products is meticulously crafted with the intention of enhancing your confidence and allure, resulting in an undeniable ability to captivate and engage anyone in your presence.

FOUNDER

The story of Lucent began with an enigmatic perfumer named Aris Zacky. Zacky, a quiet and introspective soul, was inherently gifted in the art of encapsulating the essence of emotions within fragrance.
